What is My Shop?

My Shop is a desktop application built for speed and simplicity. It allows you to track sales, manage inventory, and view reports without needing an internet connection.

Blazing Fast

Built with lightweight technologies to ensure instant load times and snappy interactions.

Offline First

No internet? No problem. All your data is stored locally and securely on your machine.

Mobile Ready

Responsive design that works perfectly on tablets and small touch screens.

Smart Dashboard

Get a bird's eye view of your business. Track today's sales, gross profit, expenses, and low stock alerts instantly.

  • Real-time sales tracking
  • Profit & Loss analysis
  • Visual sales trends

Inventory Management

Manage your products with ease. Add stock, track quantity, and categorize items like Smartphones, Accessories, and more.

  • Low stock alerts
  • Image upload support
  • Barcode scanning ready

Point of Sale (POS)

Process sales in seconds. Select products, add to cart, and print invoices tailored to your business needs.

  • Quick search
  • Mobile-friendly cart
  • Supports multiple payment methods

Sales History & Reports

Never lose track of a transaction. View detailed sales history, filter by date, and export reports to PDF.

  • Date range filtering
  • Invoice re-printing
  • Export to Excel/PDF
